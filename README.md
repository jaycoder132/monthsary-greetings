# Happy Monthsary 🎀

A romantic surprise website to celebrate your monthsary with your girlfriend. Features a beautiful pink & white design, floating hearts animation, and auto-playing music.

![Preview](https://via.placeholder.com/500x300/ffe4e6/db2777?text=Happy+Monthsary)

---

## ✨ Features

- 💕 Beautiful pink & white aesthetic
- 🎵 Music auto-plays when she clicks to begin
- 🌊 Floating hearts background animation
- 📱 Fully responsive design
- 🎂 Easy to customize months & message

## 🚀 How to Use

### 1. Add Your Song
Rename your MP3 file to `YOUR_SONG.mp3` and place it in the same folder as `index.html`.

> **Tip:** If you want to keep your original filename, open `index.html` and find line 45:
> ```html
> <audio id="music" src="YOUR_SONG.mp3" autoplay></audio>
> ```
> Change `YOUR_SONG.mp3` to your actual filename.

### 2. Customize the Message
Open `index.html` and find lines 54-58:

```html
<p class="message">
    Every moment with you has been a blessing. 
    Thank you for being my favorite person, my love, 
    and my greatest joy. Here's to many more months 
    of love and laughter together! 🥰
</p>
